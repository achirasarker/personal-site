About Me

Hi! I am a Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Waterloo focused on hardware, embedded systems and programming. I'm passionate about the applications of robotics and artifical intelligence in cutting edge devices to improve user accessibility. I want to help design products and systems that can provide inviduals with better opportunities and experiences.


I volunteer with organizations like Plan Canada and the Canadian Cancer Society to encourage and empower other young people to pursue their dreams.
